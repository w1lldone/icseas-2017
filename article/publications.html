Al-Jamiah

Published by the Al-Jami'ah Research Centre of State Islamic University Sunan Kalijaga Yogyakarta since 1962, Al-Jamiah serves as a forum for the study of Islam in Indonesia and other parts of the world, covering textual and fieldwork studies on law, philosophy, mysticism, history, art, theology, and more. The journal is indexed in DOAJ.
