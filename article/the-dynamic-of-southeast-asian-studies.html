  2. Jurnal Sain Veteriner
  3. Philippine Journal of Science*
  4. Knowledge E*
  5. Humaniora
  6. Kawistara
  7. Mimbar Hukum*

  9. Indonesian Journal of Islam and Muslim Societies*
