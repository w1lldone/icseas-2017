ASEAN Journal on Science and Technology for Development

The ASEAN Journal on Science and Technology for Development is a refereed journal of the ASEAN Committee on Science and Technology (ASEAN COST), reporting on science and technology policies and programmes, and research activities undertaken by COST in support of social and economic development of the ASEAN countries.

Articles are currently being screened for publication in this journal.
